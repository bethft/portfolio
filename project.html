Project Overview

About FridgeMinder

FridgeMinder is a trio of devices designed to transform how users manage food in their kitchens, with an emphasis on avoiding food waste. Our trio includes a magnetized fridge-front touch-screen, a camera for the inside of the refrigerator, and a mobile application.we developed FridgeMinder, a high-tech, user-friendly solution compatible with any refrigerator.

Define

Problem

“How can we help people eliminate food waste?” We wanted to create a simple solution that can seamlessly integrate into anyone's kitchen while also incorporating smart devices.

(02)

Research

Competitive Analysis

We conducted a competitive analysis that included the Oco security camera, the Smarter FridgeCam, and the Samsung Smart Fridge.

  • The functionality of the Oco security camera was limited; it tracked the contents of a refrigerator without registering the items.
  • The Smarter FridgeCam did register items, but was only available for Amazon Fresh users.
  • The Samsung Smart Fridge had an integrated touch screen and registered the contents of a refrigerator, but was very expensive and difficult to move from rental to rental.
(04)

Ideate

Solution

We provide a comprehensive solution to help users prevent food waste. Our product empowers users to avoid food waste by helping them not overbuy while shopping, using our app’s on-the-go fridge camera access or inventory of their items with expiration dates. The fridge-front touch-screen allows users at home to review the contents and expiry dates of their items without having to scour their refrigerator and examine each item. Best of all, this solution is 100% portable, allowing users to pay once and enjoy it in every dorm, apartment, and home they move on to.

Ideate

Information Architecture

We worked as a team to organize and structure our user interface to make it intuitive and easy to use. We decided to keep navigation for our landing page simple to avoid overwhelming our user, and to allow users to drill down to find more specific actions or information.

(07)

Prototype

Mid Fidelity

Our Mid-Fidelity prototype used simple strokes in designing buttons and font style. We introduced some colors to improve the accessibility of our main function to help us do further user testing to collect more feedback from our potential users.

(08)

Test

Usability Testing

User testing revealed a few weak points. Users could not immediately navigate back to their profile page, there was a learning curve for deleting and adding items, and the buttons weren’t very obvious. We altered our navigation paths and increased the brightness of our buttons by adding color.

In Reflection

When we began this project, our aim was to tackle food waste. Initially, we were eager to pack our product with as many features as possible. But through research, user feedback, and continuous refinement, we learned that a simpler approach, focused on what users truly needed, was more effective.

If we had more time, we would deepen our exploration into user experience. Specifically, we aim to gain a better understanding of the interaction between users and the FridgeMinder camera and digital display. This insight would be crucial for identifying necessary adjustments and enhancements.
